Appeal for donation to charitable organizations


Over the years PeaZip project gained some focus, and this gave to me the opportunity to turn the project's perceived value into something even more positive, and useful.

As the project itself does not need funding, being maintained for passion and for learning purpose, I'll be very glad if you consider to donate to charitable organizations which need and deserve your help far more than myself.

Following organizations are unrelated to PeaZip project, links bring to donation / fund raising pages of the official web domains of the listed organizations. NOTE: in many countries charity donations to non-profit organizations can be deducted from taxable income.

LA NINNA

Italian wildlife rescue center, specialized in sheltering and healing injuried hedgehogs, precious animals for protecting biodiversity and the forest ecosystem.
To help "La Ninna" please see the donation page.

UNICEF


The United Nations Children's Fund (UNICEF) provides long-term humanitarian and developmental assistance to children and mothers in developing countries.
Learn more about how to support UNICEF.

WWF


World Wide Fund for Nature is an international non-governmental organization for wilderness preservation and reduction of human impact on the environment.
Please read how to support WWF.

UNESCO


United Nations Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) is a specialized agency of the United Nations to contribute to peace and security by promoting international collaboration through education, science, and culture; UNESCO Online Donations.
See also: UNESCO World Heritage


PeaZip is Free Software and would likely not exist without third parties technologies also released as Free Software, I'll be glad if you consider to donate to respective Authors.
But in first and foremost place a big thank you to Lazarus and FreePascal teams, providing the cross-platform IDE that makes possible creating PeaZip and porting it to so many different platforms.
You may also consider about donate to Electronic Frontier Foundation, Free Software Foundation and Open Source Initiative: the Free Software movement stands to protect user's and developer's rights to freely share works, experiences and ideas.

 Please note that this list doesn't aim to be exhaustive, nor to give a sort of "ranking" of merit between various organizations and causes.
Fund rising is vital to many organizations: if you are visiting this page to give an economic contribution to PeaZip project, please consider, instead, to donate that amount to any other organization or cause you believe in, listed or not on this page.
Thank you very much!

Synopsis:  Donation page, support suggested charity organizations, or the Free Software movement and the Open Source projects making PeaZip pssible. Thanks in advance!
